Artihm Whitepaper

Version 1.0 · May 2025

Abstract

Artihm is a decentralized AI-driven NFT creation and deployment platform. By combining artificial intelligence with blockchain technology, Artihm allows users to generate unique, verifiably scarce digital artworks (NFTs) autonomously and deploy them on-chain. The ART token powers the ecosystem, enabling governance, minting, and incentivizing participation.

Vision

To democratize creativity by giving anyone access to generative AI tools that produce high-quality art and collectibles—fully secured, owned, and monetized via blockchain.

Key Features

  • AI-Generated Art: AI models trained on diverse art styles and inputs generate unique NFT visuals and metadata.
  • NFT Deployment: Instantly deploy artworks to EVM-compatible blockchains with smart contracts.
  • Rarity Engine: Built-in rarity and trait combinatorics ensure each piece is unique and collectible.
  • NFT Studio: No-code interface to create, preview, and mint collections.
  • DAO Governance: ART token holders govern key decisions including model training direction, platform fees, and treasury use.
  • Creator Rewards: Revenue-sharing and staking models for those who contribute training data or prompt templates.
